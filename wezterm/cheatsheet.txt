
  WezTerm Keybindings  (leader: Ctrl+Space)

  Panes
    \          split horizontal
    -          split vertical
    h j k l    navigate panes
    z          zoom pane
    x          close pane

  Tabs
    t          new tab
    , .        prev / next tab

  Workspaces
    w          workspace picker
    n          new workspace
    [ ]        prev / next workspace

  Other
    Enter      new window
    ?          this cheat sheet


  Press q to close.
